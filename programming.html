Piano Fingering Generation with Linear CRFs

Built a system that, given unfingered sequences of notes, generated fingerings for those notes. The idea was that this system could be used to help beginner piano students learn to play a new piece of music. We decided to use a variable-based model, specifically a chain-structure factor graph called a linear-chain conditional random field (CRF). Our finished system generated physically possible sets of fingerings that were comparable to professionally determined fingerings.
